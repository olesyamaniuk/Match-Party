Feature keys

  • Immersive Gameplay:

    Unlike traditional match games, Match Party - Tile 3D lets you interact with objects in a fully 3D environment, offering a new dimension of challenge.

  • Exciting Puzzle Mechanics:

    Rotate, sort, and organize tiles from different angles to spot hidden matches faster.

  • Dynamic Boosters & Power-Ups:

    Shuffle Boosters – If you're stuck, reshuffle the board to find new matching possibilities.

  • Daily Rewards & Special Events:

    Explore and conquer hundreds of exciting levels, each offering unique challenges and opportunities to master your skills.

  • Compete & Share Your Progress:

    Connect to leaderboards, challenge your friends, and track your progress as you become a match-3D master.

  • Offline Play Mode:

    Enjoy seamless gameplay anywhere, even without an internet connection.

About us

  • At Clever Duck, we believe in crafting highly engaging, visually appealing, and innovativepuzzle games that challenge players in new and exciting ways.
  • With Match Party - Tile 3D, we have redefined the match-3 puzzle genre by introducing a fully interactive 3D world. Players can rotate, move, and analyze objects in a way that enhances problem-solving skills, strategic planning, and hand-eye coordination.
  • Why We Created Match Party - Tile 3D:
  • To offer a fresh take on traditional tile-matching puzzles.
  • To create visually stunning 3D gameplay that feels immersive and dynamic.
  • To allow players to customize their puzzle experience with power-ups and strategic tools.
